About Us

In a society now nurtured by iPads, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and various forms of potentially damaging media, improving literacy while incorporating the inevitable encroachment of technology is essential for kids. Our Capstone project will target promoting mental health and the significance of children's literature through diverse, inclusive, and engaging media. Specifically, we strive to aid multilingual learners, those with learning disabilities like ADHD, and decreased attention spans to improve their sociability, reading, and critical thinking skills. Our team, Sean Savadian, Sydney Clinkscales, and Maggie Clark Zavala, created a website that is both accessible, diverse, and welcoming to all young readers.
